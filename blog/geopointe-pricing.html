Request a comparable Geopointe quote

Ask Geopointe for the annual licence total at your current and planned seat count. The quote should identify the included mapping, territory, routing, mobile, API, support, and implementation scope.

Calculate Tourvia from the published rate

Tourvia costs €30 excluding tax per licensed user per month, billed annually. The annual formula is licensed users × €360 excluding tax. Ten users cost €3,600 per year, 20 cost €7,200, and 50 cost €18,000.

Do not compare those totals with an estimated Geopointe rate. Use the current quote, one currency, and equivalent requirements.

Items to include beside the licence price

Ask both vendors about:

  • Implementation and admin setup time
  • Training for field reps and managers
  • Salesforce data flow, external processing, and integration maintenance
  • Mobile data usage and device support
  • Contract, renewal, support, and exit terms
