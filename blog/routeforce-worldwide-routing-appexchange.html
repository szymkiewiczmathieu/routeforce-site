What Tourvia makes clearer

Tourvia is not only a mapping layer. It is a Salesforce-native field execution product built for teams that need route planning, visit execution, and business workflows inside the same environment.

The product story is easier to understand publicly: teams can install Tourvia from a 30-day trial request, validate the native workflow, and then subscribe to keep full access when they need more advanced execution.

Global routing remains a strong differentiator

One of Tourvia’s strongest product signals is the ability to support large-scale routing scenarios while staying native to Salesforce.

Tourvia operates its routing engine worldwide, so route optimization is not limited to a single country or region. Teams operating across Europe, the Americas, Asia-Pacific, or any combination of territories get the same optimization quality. There is no geographic lock-in.

For teams planning visits across multiple territories or countries, global routing matters because field execution does not stop at a city boundary. The product story becomes stronger when route planning, visit creation, and execution all stay connected to Salesforce records.

Why the product positioning is stronger now

The clearer positioning is not only about routing. It is about the full operating model for field teams:

  • Salesforce-native workflow instead of a disconnected external tool
  • AppExchange entry point that reduces adoption friction
  • One subscription for the entire feature set
  • Field execution quality with visit reports, GPS-verified check-in, and mobile use inside Salesforce

EU data hosting and routing infrastructure

Tourvia's routing infrastructure is hosted in France. That means route calculations and geographic processing stay within the EU, which can help organizations that want tighter control over where routing-related data is processed.

For European teams evaluating field tools, this is a practical differentiator compared to products that route data through US-based services without clear data residency commitments.

What teams can highlight during evaluation

For a Salesforce admin or revenue operations lead, the value is straightforward: Tourvia can start as an installable native app, then expand into a broader field execution system without switching platforms.

Tourvia is distributed through private AppExchange access coordinated with the customer’s Salesforce admin. A requested 30-day trial includes route optimization, GPS check-in, and configurable visit reports. The subscription is EUR 30 excluding tax per licensed user per month, billed annually, with custom quotes available for larger enterprise deployments.

That makes internal rollout easier to defend than products that require a separate field stack from day one.

What Tourvia supports in the field

Tourvia supports the full field workflow inside Salesforce:

  • configurable workflows powered by Screen Flows and Lightning Web Components

Conclusion

Tourvia offers a clear product position: Salesforce-native route planning and field execution, global routing capacity, and a simpler adoption path from AppExchange install to paid rollout.
