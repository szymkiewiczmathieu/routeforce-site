Start with the workflow, not the category label

Geopointe is an AppExchange managed package with mapping, geocoding, territory, and routing capabilities. Verify the exact plan, route constraints, mobile workflow, data processing, and reporting required by your team.

What to look for in a Geopointe alternative

If your main goal is to turn Salesforce records into routes and visits, prioritize these features:

  • Salesforce managed-package workflow and documented external processing
  • Route optimization for multiple stops per day
  • Mobile app for field reps with offline resilience
  • Check-in and visit reporting tied to Salesforce Events
  • Transparent annual pricing

Tourvia vs Geopointe at a glance

Both Tourvia and Geopointe are AppExchange managed packages. Compare the objects used, route constraints, Event creation, mobile visits, reporting, and data flow directly in each product.

Tourvia builds the route from Accounts, Contacts, Leads, or Opportunities, optimizes the sequence, and creates Events in Salesforce. The mobile app then guides reps through the day, records check-ins, and writes the visit report back into the CRM.

Run the same representative route and visit-reporting scenario in both products rather than inferring capability from positioning copy.

Geopointe asks buyers to request current pricing. Tourvia publishes €30 excluding tax per licensed user per month, billed annually. Compare the final Geopointe quote and Tourvia total at the same seat count, currency, and feature scope.

When to choose Tourvia over Geopointe

Include Tourvia when the requirement centres on routes, Salesforce Events, mobile visits, and reports. Include Geopointe when mapping, territory, and geo-analytics requirements are central. Test both when the scope overlaps.
