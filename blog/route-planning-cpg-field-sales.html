The CPG field sales challenge

Consumer packaged goods sales teams cover dense routes of retail outlets. Each visit matters for shelf placement, promotions, and order taking. Poor routing means missed stores and lower revenue per rep.

What CPG route planning needs

  • Route optimization across many small accounts
  • Visit frequency planning by store tier
  • Check-in and check-out tracking
  • Photo or note capture tied to each visit
  • Coverage reporting by territory and rep

Why native Salesforce matters for CPG

Retail accounts, contacts, and opportunities already live in Salesforce. A native route planning tool uses that data without sync, exports, or duplicate records. Managers see coverage in real time.
