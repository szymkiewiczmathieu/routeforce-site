Route planning for pharmaceutical sales teams inside Salesforce

Plan visits to healthcare professionals from Salesforce records, create the Events, and keep the resulting activity available for the organisation's own reporting and compliance process.

Published 2026-07-19 · 7 min

Start with the visit constraints already held in Salesforce

Healthcare visits can involve appointment windows, site-access rules, target priorities, call notes, and territory assignments. The route should use the fields and records approved by the organisation rather than a generic list of addresses.

  • Strict visit windows and hospital access rules
  • High-value targets that must be visited on schedule
  • Call notes and sample management tied to each visit
  • Territory alignment with sales targets
  • Compliance with local pharma regulations

Test the full data flow

Tourvia is installed as a Salesforce managed package. Salesforce remains the system of record, while the minimum coordinates and timing constraints required for route calculation are processed by Tourvia infrastructure in France as described in the DPA.

The product can create Salesforce Events and record GPS-assisted check-in information. Those records support the customer's reporting process; Tourvia does not determine regulatory compliance.
