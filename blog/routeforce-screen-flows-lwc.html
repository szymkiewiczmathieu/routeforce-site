RouteForce + Screen Flows + LWC: extensibility for field teams

RouteForce does more than display a map. Custom Actions can trigger Salesforce Screen Flows with embedded Lightning Web Components directly from the map, making advanced business workflows possible without breaking the native experience.

Why this matters

For advanced Salesforce teams, the real question is not only whether a route planning tool works. The more important question is whether it can adapt to business workflows without creating a heavy integration project.

With RouteForce, the answer can be yes. Custom Actions let field reps launch business workflows directly from the map by combining Screen Flows and Lightning Web Components.

What it looks like in practice

A field rep taps a store, account, or lead on the map and instantly opens a custom business workflow such as product availability checks, price surveys, shelf audits, or visit-specific forms.

Simple architecture, powerful result

  • RouteForce handles the map and triggers the action
  • Screen Flow acts as the container and receives context, including recordId
  • LWC delivers the business UX: filters, sorting, quick inputs, save actions

The result is a native Salesforce route planning product that stays packageable while remaining flexible for in-house Salesforce teams.
