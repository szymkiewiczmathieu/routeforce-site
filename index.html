Open the map. The whole territory reports in.

Every geocoded record appears on one live map, filtered by any Salesforce field, clustered by density and colored by status. Saved presets put the same view in front of the whole team.

  • checkMulti-object map, clustering, heatmap

Lasso the stops. The engine orders the day.

Draw an area or pick records one by one, then let the Tourvia routing engine sequence the route. It accounts for the start point, time windows, lunch break, tolls and highway preferences. The demo day: 5 stops, 38.0 km, 1h11 of driving.

  • checkOptimized order in seconds
  • checkTime windows, lunch break, toll rules
  • checkNearby suggestions fill the gaps

One click writes the calendar after flagging conflicts.

The optimized route becomes timed Salesforce Events on the right records. Tourvia checks the rep’s existing agenda and surfaces conflicts before anything is created.

  • checkTimed Event per stop, on the record

Today’s agenda runs in Salesforce Mobile. At each stop, the rep uses a GPS-assisted proximity check, completes the visit report, checks out, and moves to the next visit.

Every visit lands in reporting managers already trust.

On-site rate, visit trends and routes per rep use standard Salesforce reporting. They are included from day one and customizable like any report in your org.

Pre-flight questions

Frequently asked questions

What is Tourvia?
Tourvia is Salesforce route planning software that keeps route optimization, visit planning and field execution inside Salesforce. It is the new product name for RouteForce, with the same managed package and workflow.
Who is Tourvia best for?
Salesforce-first field teams that want route planning and visit execution tied directly to CRM records, Events and reporting instead of exporting to a separate routing app and syncing back.
Is Tourvia on the AppExchange?
Tourvia V7 has passed the Salesforce security review and is distributed through a private AppExchange listing. The listing is linked from this site so clients can open it; installation remains controlled by private access for the target org.
How much does Tourvia cost?
Tourvia costs €30 excluding tax per licensed user per month, billed annually. See full pricing details.
Where is my data processed?
Salesforce remains the system of record for accounts, Events, visits, and reports. Route calculations use only the coordinates and timing constraints required for the route. These requests are processed by Tourvia infrastructure hosted in France.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. Tourvia runs in the Salesforce Mobile App: map, today’s agenda, navigation hand-off to Google Maps, GPS check-in, and visit reports all work from the field.
How do I start a trial?
Request a 30-day trial via the contact form. We qualify your Salesforce org and share private package access. Your admin installs and configures Tourvia using the documentation.
