Works everywhere in Salesforce

RouteForce is not only a managed package you install. It is a reusable Salesforce-native component that admins can place, configure, and adapt without custom code. Learn more about native integration.

account_tree

Record page integration

The RouteForce component can live directly on Account, Lead, or Opportunity pages. It centers automatically on the current record location and loads nearby records, so reps get a contextual geographic view without leaving their workflow.

tune

Two layers of configuration

Admins control behavior with Custom Metadata and handle presentation in Lightning App Builder. Features, filters, defaults, legends, colors, and popup fields can all be adjusted without code.

widgets

Managed app, reusable everywhere

The package works immediately after install, then admins can clone pages, layouts, and reports to match their business. The LWC component is reusable across App Pages, Record Pages, Home Pages, tabs, custom apps, and Experience Cloud.

webhook

Supported targets

RouteForce is exposed for Account, Lead, and Opportunity record pages, and can also be placed on Lightning App Pages, Home Pages, Tabs, the Utility Bar, and Experience Cloud pages.
