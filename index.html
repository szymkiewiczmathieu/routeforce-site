Frequently asked questions

What is Tourvia?
Tourvia is Salesforce route planning software that keeps route optimization, visit planning, and field execution inside Salesforce. It is the new product name for RouteForce — same managed package, same workflow.
Is Tourvia on the AppExchange?
Tourvia V7 has passed the Salesforce security review and is distributed through a private AppExchange listing — installable by access. Request access via the contact form and we share the install link for your org.
How much does Tourvia cost?
Standard pricing is €30 per user per month, billed annually. Larger enterprise or multi-entity deployments are quoted separately. See full pricing details.
Where is my data processed?
Your CRM data stays in your Salesforce org — Tourvia is a native managed package with no external data sync. Route optimization requests are processed by our engine hosted on OVHcloud in France, RGPD-aligned by design.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. Tourvia runs in the Salesforce Mobile App: map, today's agenda, navigation hand-off to Google Maps, GPS check-in, and visit reports all work from the field.
How do I start a trial?
Request a 30-day trial via the contact form. We qualify your Salesforce org, share private install access, and help your admin onboard — then you confirm the paid scope before production rollout.
