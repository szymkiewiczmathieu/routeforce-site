What is Salesforce route planning?

Salesforce route planning means organizing field visits directly in your Salesforce CRM. Instead of switching between spreadsheets, mapping tools and your CRM, account data, schedules and optimized routes all live in one place.

For teams that already use Salesforce as their source of truth, native route planning removes data silos. Reps see accounts on an interactive map, select the accounts to visit and get an optimized route without leaving Salesforce.

A native Salesforce route planner typically uses the Account object, with Contact sub-queries when needed, to display customer locations on a map. It calculates the most efficient visit order based on working hours, lunch break and visit duration, then creates Salesforce Events automatically.

Why plan routes natively inside Salesforce?

Many field teams still plan their day with Google Maps, spreadsheets or standalone route planners. Those tools can calculate directions, but they disconnect route planning from CRM data.

The problem with external tools

  • Data duplication: Addresses are exported from Salesforce, imported elsewhere, then copied back.
  • No CRM context: External tools do not know account priority, last visit date or ownership.
  • Manual event creation: Reps still have to create Salesforce Events stop by stop.
  • No management visibility: Managers cannot see planned field coverage directly in Salesforce.

The advantages of native planning

  • Single source of truth: Accounts, visit history, planned routes and events stay in Salesforce.
  • CRM-aware optimization: Filter by last visit, potential, territory or any custom field before planning.
  • One-click event creation: Optimized route to Salesforce Events automatically.
  • Permission-aware: Profiles, sharing rules and field-level security are respected.
  • Mobile-ready: The same planner works in Salesforce mobile or any mobile browser.

Key capabilities of a Salesforce route planner

Interactive map with account visualization

Display Salesforce accounts on a dynamic map with smart clustering and custom color coding.

Route optimization algorithm

A real optimizer calculates the best visit sequence, not just point-to-point directions. RouteForce uses VROOM, a proven open-source Vehicle Routing Problem solver.

Automatic Salesforce Event creation

After optimization, one click creates all Salesforce calendar events with the right account, contact, time slot and address.

Smart filters

Filter map records by territory, last visit date, account type, owner, potential or any Salesforce field.

GPS-based nearby accounts

When a rep has a gap in the day, GPS geolocation shows nearby Salesforce accounts inside a configurable radius.

Route history

Every planned route is saved automatically with distance, duration, stops and date.

Export and navigation

Send the optimized route to Google Maps or Waze for navigation, or export route data as CSV.
