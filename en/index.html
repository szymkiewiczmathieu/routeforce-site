RouteForce is a managed Salesforce package — no external app, no third-party sync. It respects your profiles, permissions and sharing rules natively. Your CRM data never leaves Salesforce.

Accounts, opportunities, and leads

Use RouteForce to visualize your Salesforce objects on the map and give field teams more context for planning, prioritization, and execution.

French address enrichment

RouteForce can now enrich French addresses and populate GPS coordinates directly from the app using the French National Address Database (data.gouv.fr). This speeds up route preparation and improves map data quality.

Multi-currency

Opportunity amounts are displayed in their native currency. International teams see each deal in the correct currency — full support for Salesforce custom picklist values.

Click-to-call from the map

Phone numbers appear automatically on every map marker and every route step. One tap on mobile to call, one click on desktop to open your phone app. Works on Accounts, Leads and Opportunities. No configuration needed.

Suggested Visits — nearby accounts

At the end of your route, RouteForce shows the closest accounts you haven't visited recently — with distance and days since last visit. Accounts with no visit on record are flagged first. One click to add them to the route.

Rich hover cards

Hover a map marker to see the clickable phone number, last visit date (red if overdue), assigned contact, and a direct link to the Salesforce record. All the context you need without opening the record.

Native adoption dashboard

A Usage Analytics tab built into the Salesforce app: adoption rate, active users over 30 days, top users, action totals (routes optimized, visits created, events), and a weekly activity chart. No third-party tool needed.
