RouteForce in Action

Discover the interface directly integrated in Salesforce

Cartographie nationale & segmentation client

View all your Salesforce accounts across the entire country in seconds. The color legend instantly distinguishes Prospects, Direct Clients and Indirect Clients. The "Around me" circle shows the catchment area around your position.

Instant Client Card

Click a marker to see account details: address, owner, last visit. Create an event or launch the route without leaving the map.

Mixed Route Optimized Across Paris

Customer accounts 📍 and leads ⭐ on the same route. The algorithm optimizes the visit order based on your time constraints. Each stop shows its associated contact.

Select Accounts to Visit

Zoom into your area, click accounts to include in the route. The side panel shows planning parameters: departure time, lunch break, end of day, visit duration. Click "Optimize route" to start the calculation.

Nearby Accounts — Real-time GPS

One tap on the 📍 button and RouteForce immediately displays all Salesforce accounts within a configurable radius around you. Perfect for impromptu visits.

Route History

All your past routes saved automatically. Reload a route from last week in one click — distance, duration and stops retrieved instantly.

Complete Planning Interface

The "Plan a route" panel brings everything together: starting point (GPS position or Salesforce account), date, schedules, lunch break, visit duration. The "Nearby Accounts" section shows accounts near your route, ready to be added.

💾 Save Your Routes

After planning and optimization, name and save your route. Find it in the history to re-run it the following week — same route, same accounts, zero effort. Ideal for recurring routes.

⭐ Accounts + Leads on the Same Map

Plan your routes with existing accounts and leads to prospect — on the same map. Leads appear as stars ⭐, accounts as colored pins 📍. One click on "Create visits" creates calendar events in Salesforce, with the right associated contact.

📅 Field Agenda — Day Of

Your today's appointments appear directly on the map with 📅 markers. The side panel shows the agenda: next meeting highlighted, one-click check-in, edit and delete on the fly. No more switching between calendar and map.

⚙️ 17 Settings — Zero Code

Everything is configurable via Custom Metadata Types: display mode, GPS radius, feature toggles, default zoom, cluster radius... A guided wizard sets it all up in 5 steps. Admins keep full control without touching code.
