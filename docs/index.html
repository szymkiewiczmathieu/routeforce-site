What is new in TourviaApp 7.26.0

  • · Today's Agenda — tap-to-call contacts: events linked to a contact now show the contact's name and a clickable phone link (mobile or office, with FaceTime/Teams/dialer fallback). Works on Account events that have a Contact attendee too.
  • · Campaign as route entry: a 6th inline entry point lets field reps plan a tour straight from a Salesforce Campaign — pick the Campaign and all its geocoded members land as route steps in one click.
  • · Three new Permission Set Groups: TourviaApp_User_Group / Manager_Group / Admin_Group, plus compound Address, geolocation and phone FLS granted out of the box on Account, Contact and Lead.
