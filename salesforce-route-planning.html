Why keep route planning inside Salesforce?

Standalone route planners can calculate a route, but they introduce another login and a separate data model. Tourvia keeps the operational record in Salesforce.

  • Current CRM context: plan from owners, priorities, Campaign status, previous visits, and any other Salesforce field.
  • No duplicate visit entry: planned visits become Salesforce Events on the correct records.
  • Native access control: the package includes Permission Set Groups for users, managers, and admins.
  • Native reporting: visits, route history, check-ins, and reports remain available to Salesforce analytics.

What Tourvia accounts for when building a route

A field day is more than the shortest line between pins. Tourvia can include:

  • start and end locations;
  • working hours and lunch breaks;
  • customer availability windows;
  • visit duration at each stop;
  • travel mode and route preferences;
  • existing Salesforce Event conflicts.

The rep can review the result before any Event is created.

Salesforce remains the system of record

Accounts, Events, visits, route history, and reports are stored in Salesforce. Route calculations use only the coordinates and timing constraints required to calculate the route. Those requests are processed by Tourvia infrastructure hosted in France.
