Calculate the annual cost before the trial

Tourvia has one public per-user price: €30 excluding tax per licensed user per month, billed annually. That is €1,800/year for 5 users, €3,600/year for 10 users, and €7,200/year for 20 users.

The subscription includes the complete product and route calculations. Your Salesforce admin handles installation and configuration with the published documentation.

What the subscription includes

  • Complete 30-day trial on request for qualified Salesforce orgs.
  • Tourvia subscription: €30/user/month excluding tax, billed annually.
  • Installation: handled by the customer’s Salesforce admin using the documentation.
  • Route calculations included: no routing API fee is passed through by Tourvia.

Hidden costs to check before buying

  • Routing API costs: are map/routing calls included or passed through?
  • Premium route optimization: is optimization included or gated behind a higher tier?
  • Managers and admins: do occasional users need paid seats?
  • Salesforce workflow: are visits, events, reports, and permissions native or synced through an external app?
