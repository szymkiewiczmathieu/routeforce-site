Why teams search for flat-rate route optimization

“Flat-rate route optimization” is usually shorthand for a simple buying requirement: predictable pricing. Many mapping products combine per-user fees, premium tiers, API limits, and add-ons that make the real cost harder to forecast.

Tourvia does not claim a flat-org subscription anymore. The current public model is explicit: €30/user/month excluding tax, billed annually.

Tourvia current pricing

  • 30-day trial on request for initial evaluation.
  • Tourvia Pro: €30/user/month excluding tax, billed annually.
  • Setup: self-setup or managed setup when the customer wants delivery support.
  • No routing API pass-through: Tourvia uses self-hosted routing infrastructure based on VROOM and OpenRouteService.

Hidden costs to check before buying

  • Routing API costs: are map/routing calls included or passed through?
  • Premium route optimization: is optimization included or gated behind a higher tier?
  • Managers and admins: do occasional users need paid seats?
  • Salesforce workflow: are visits, events, reports, and permissions native or synced through an external app?
